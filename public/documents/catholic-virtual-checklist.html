Virtual Book Fair Checklist

Your step-by-step guide to a successful Catholic virtual book fair

STEP 1 You Did It!

  • Choose Ignatius Book Fairs and set your virtual fair dates You've already accomplished this step! Check out our Resources throughout this guide.

STEP 2 Advertise Early & Often

  • Advertising is the biggest predictor of success — you cannot over-advertise a virtual fair!
  • Use your social media and email lists to spread the word; our Advertising Resources make it effortless
  • Create your Book Fair Coordinator account
  • Invite teachers to claim classrooms, make reading recommendations, and set up wish lists Confirm all teachers have logged in before parents start creating accounts

STEP 3 Share Links

  • Distribute the Digital Flyer link to all parents and parishioners
  • Share the Shopping Website link widely

STEP 4 Set a Sales Goal

  • Set a sales goal and communicate it to your community

STEP 5 Display Sample Books

  • If you ordered a sample box, set up books in the main foyer or a prominent location
  • Make the display inviting: use the colorful tablecloth, add balloons or twinkle lights
  • Add signage with fair dates and how the school benefits from orders

STEP 6 Fun Incentives

  • Plan a fun event or incentive to get students excited: art contest, guessing game, reading day, or similar Prizes can be free books purchased with your Ave Dollars rewards

STEP 7 Receive & Distribute Orders

  • Orders arrive labeled by customer name ~7–10 days after fair ends
  • Distribute books or notify customers for pickup

STEP 8 Spend Your Rewards

  • Log in to check your growing Ave Dollars balance (updates daily)
  • Start spending rewards immediately — available right away Virtual fair rewards are in Ave Dollars only (not convertible to cash)

Advertising Schedule

  • 7 weeks before: Invite teachers to claim classrooms and create wish lists Videos: Create Coordinator Account • Invite Teachers
  • 6 weeks before: Post Save the Date on all social media, parish bulletin, and newsletter
  • 5 weeks before: Share this video on social media; include sign-up link for parents to create accounts
  • 4 weeks before: Post another Save the Date reminder with this graphic
  • 3 weeks before: Highlight select titles your staff or children enjoy; explain how sales benefit the school
  • 2 weeks before: Have principal or pastor send the Letter to Parents
  • 1 week before: Edit and send home the backpack flyer (1 per family) Can be inserted inside the catalog you receive
  • Evening before: Post kickoff graphic on social media Videos: How to Create a Parent Account • How Orders Benefit Your School

During the Fair (Daily Posts)

  • Day 1: Kick off with the Reading Challenge announcement
  • Day 2: Post this graphic; remind community about free shipping to school during fair dates + online ordering link
  • Day 3: Highlight reading recommendations by age/grade and teacher wish lists
  • Day 4: Share staff top picks for fun, interesting, and thought-provoking books
  • Day 5: Encourage "Stop, Drop and Read" — 20 min daily family reading habit
  • Day 6: Thank supporters; ask for book recommendations from parents
  • Day 7: Final day reminder — last chance to order; mention 30% earnings for school

FINAL Have a Wonderful Virtual Book Fair!

Reach out to your Book Fair Manager if any issues arise — we're here to cheer you on and give you advice and support along the way!
